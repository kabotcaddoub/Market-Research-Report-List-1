<p><p>The pedestal drill market comprises two main types: floor pedestal drills and benchtop pedestal drills. Floor pedestal drills are larger, designed for industrial settings, providing stability and power for heavy-duty drilling tasks. They typically feature a robust base and can accommodate larger workpieces. In contrast, benchtop pedestal drills are compact and ideal for smaller workshops, offering portability and ease of use. They are suitable for light to medium-duty drilling tasks, making them popular among hobbyists and small business owners.</p></p>
