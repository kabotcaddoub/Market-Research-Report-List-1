<p><p>The Human Metapneumovirus Nucleic Acid Test Kit market includes two main types: Dry PCR and Fluorescence PCR. Dry PCR involves amplifying viral genetic material without the need for reagents, thereby simplifying the process and enhancing stability for transport and storage. In contrast, Fluorescence PCR utilizes fluorescent dyes to monitor the amplification process in real-time, allowing for rapid detection and quantification of the virus. Together, these technologies facilitate efficient diagnosis and management of severe respiratory infections caused by metapneumovirus.</p></p>
