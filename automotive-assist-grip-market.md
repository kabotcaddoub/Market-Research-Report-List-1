<p><p>The automotive assist grip market is categorized into three main types: fixed type, extension type, and rotating type. Fixed type grips are permanently mounted and provide stability for entry and exit. Extension type grips can be adjusted to accommodate different user heights or preferences, enhancing accessibility. Rotating type grips offer adaptability by allowing the handle to pivot, making them easier to grasp from various angles. Each type serves to improve vehicle safety and ease of use for passengers and drivers.</p></p>
