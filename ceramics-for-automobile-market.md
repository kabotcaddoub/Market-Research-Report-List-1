<p><ul><li>Structural Ceramics</li><li>Functional Ceramics</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ceramics market for automobiles is divided into two main types: structural ceramics and functional ceramics. Structural ceramics provide mechanical strength and durability, used for components like engine parts and brake systems, enhancing overall vehicle performance. In contrast, functional ceramics focus on specific properties such as electrical insulation, heat resistance, or wear resistance, often found in sensors, catalytic converters, and other electronic systems. Together, these ceramics improve vehicle safety, efficiency, and longevity, driving advancements in automotive engineering.</p></p>
