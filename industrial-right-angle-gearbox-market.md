Worm gears provide excellent torque multiplication and are ideal for low-speed, high-load scenarios due to their unique design. Hypoid gears combine the advantages of bevel and worm gears, allowing for smooth operation and high efficiency at various angles. These gear types cater to diverse industries, enhancing performance and reliability in machinery.</p></p>
