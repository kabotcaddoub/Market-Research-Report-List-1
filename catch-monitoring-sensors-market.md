In contrast, dual frequency sensors utilize both 40 kHz and 70 kHz, allowing for enhanced resolution and accuracy in detecting various species and sizes of fish. This dual approach increases the versatility and effectiveness of catch monitoring, aiding in better resource management and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
