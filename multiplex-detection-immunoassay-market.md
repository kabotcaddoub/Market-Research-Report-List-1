<p><p>The multiplex detection immunoassay market encompasses three main types: nucleic acid-based techniques, protein-based multiplex techniques, and biosensor-based techniques. Nucleic acid-based techniques involve the simultaneous detection of multiple DNA or RNA targets, enhancing genetic analysis and pathogen detection. Protein-based multiplex techniques utilize antibodies to simultaneously quantify various proteins, aiding in disease diagnosis and biomarker discovery. Biosensor-based techniques integrate biological recognition elements with signal transduction, enabling real-time analysis and improved sensitivity in detecting multiple analytes in complex samples.</p></p>
