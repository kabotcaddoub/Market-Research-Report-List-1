<p><strong>The Plaque Modification Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Thrombectomy Devices</li><li>Atherectomy Devices</li><li>CTO Devices</li><li>EPD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plaque modification devices market includes several specialized categories. Thrombectomy devices are designed to remove blood clots from vessels, improving blood flow. Atherectomy devices specifically target and remove plaque buildup in arteries to restore normal circulation. Chronic Total Occlusion (CTO) devices focus on treating completely blocked arteries, enabling revascularization procedures. Finally, Embolic Protection Devices (EPDs) are utilized during these interventions to capture and prevent debris from traveling downstream, enhancing patient safety during plaque modification procedures.</p></p>
