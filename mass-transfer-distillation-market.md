<p><p>Mass transfer in distillation involves separating components from liquid mixtures based on differences in volatility. The market for distillation technologies includes various column internals essential for optimizing efficiency. Random packing offers large surface areas for vapor-liquid interactions, enhancing mass transfer. Structured packing provides even better performance with a design that maximizes contact while minimizing pressure drop. Trays, on the other hand, facilitate effective stagewise separation by allowing vapor to rise through liquid pools, making them suitable for larger volume processes.</p></p>
