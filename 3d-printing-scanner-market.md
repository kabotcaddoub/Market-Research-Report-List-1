<p><p>The 3D printing scanner market can be categorized into short-range, medium-range, and long-range scanners, each targeting specific applications. Short-range scanners excel in capturing fine details and textures, suitable for small objects and intricate designs. Medium-range scanners balance detail with coverage, often used for larger items or assemblies. Long-range scanners focus on extensive environments or large objects, providing broad coverage with less detail, and are ideal for architectural projects or topography mapping, effectively catering to diverse user needs.</p></p>
