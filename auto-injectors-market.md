<p><p>The auto injectors market encompasses several types based on fuel injection technologies. SFI (Sequential Fuel Injection) involves injecting fuel into each cylinder sequentially for improved efficiency. GDI (Gasoline Direct Injection) directly injects fuel into the combustion chamber, enhancing performance and fuel economy. SFI + GDI combines both methods, optimizing engine performance and emissions. DDI (Diesel Direct Injection) targets diesel engines, using direct fuel injection for better efficiency and lower emissions. Each type caters to different engine requirements and advancements in automotive technology.</p></p>
