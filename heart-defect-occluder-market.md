<p><p>The heart defect occluder market encompasses devices designed to close specific congenital heart defects. Patent Ductus Arteriosus (PDA) occluders treat abnormal connections between the aorta and pulmonary artery. Atrial Septal Defect (ASD) occluders address holes between the heart's upper chambers, preventing blood from flowing incorrectly. Ventricular Septal Defect (VSD) occluders target openings between the heart's lower chambers, ensuring proper blood circulation. Each type of occluder plays a crucial role in improving cardiac function and patient outcomes.</p></p>
