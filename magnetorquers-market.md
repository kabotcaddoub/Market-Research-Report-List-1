<p><p>Magnetorquers are crucial components in satellite attitude control systems, utilizing magnetic fields to manipulate a spacecraft's orientation. The air-core magnetorquer consists of coils without a ferromagnetic core, offering lightweight and efficient operation. Embedded coils integrate directly into satellite structures for space-saving designs, enhancing reliability. Torquerods employ a rod-like structure with coils wrapped around them, providing high magnetic torque in a compact form. Each type caters to specific mission requirements, influencing performance, weight, and installation flexibility in satellite design.</p></p>
