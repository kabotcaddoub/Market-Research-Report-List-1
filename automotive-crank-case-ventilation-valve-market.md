<p><p>The Automotive Crank Case Ventilation Valve market is primarily divided into two types: Plunger Type and Diaphragm Type. Plunger Type valves utilize a moving plunger to regulate airflow and prevent excessive pressure within the crankcase, offering reliability and responsiveness. In contrast, Diaphragm Type valves employ a flexible diaphragm to maintain constant ventilation, ensuring optimal performance and emission control. Both types contribute to engine efficiency and longevity by managing crankcase gases, thereby preventing oil contamination and enhancing overall vehicle performance.</p></p>
