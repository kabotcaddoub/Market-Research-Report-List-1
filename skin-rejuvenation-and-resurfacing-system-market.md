<p><p>Skin rejuvenation and resurfacing systems are categorized into two main types: ablative and non-ablative lasers. Ablative lasers remove the outer layers of skin, promoting new collagen growth and skin tightening, making them effective for deep wrinkles and significant skin damage. In contrast, non-ablative lasers target the deeper layers of skin without damaging the surface, yielding less invasive treatments that minimize recovery time. Both types are used to improve skin texture, tone, and overall appearance.</p></p>
