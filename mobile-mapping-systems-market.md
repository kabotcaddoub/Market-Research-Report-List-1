<p><ul><li>Direct Mobile Mapping System</li><li>Backpack Mobile Mapping System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mobile Mapping Systems (MMS) are categorized into Direct Mobile Mapping Systems and Backpack Mobile Mapping Systems. Direct Mobile Mapping Systems utilize vehicles equipped with advanced sensors and GPS to capture geospatial data efficiently while traveling. Conversely, Backpack Mobile Mapping Systems involve portable setups carried by individuals, allowing for detailed surveys in crowded or complex environments that vehicles cannot access. Both types enhance data collection capabilities, offering flexibility for various applications in urban planning, infrastructure management, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
