<p><strong>The Automotive Axle Parts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-Floating Axle Parts</li><li>Semi-Floating Axle Parts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive axle parts market is primarily categorized into full-floating and semi-floating axle parts. Full-floating axles feature an independent wheel assembly, where the axle is supported by bearings in the housing, allowing for greater load capacity and stability. They are commonly used in heavy-duty vehicles. In contrast, semi-floating axles have the axle shaft supporting both the wheel and the differential, making them simpler and lighter, suitable for lighter trucks and passenger vehicles. Each type serves specific performance and application needs.</p></p>
