<p><p>Embedded Single Board Computers (SBC) are categorized into several types based on their architecture and application. CPCI (CompactPCI) is used for industrial and telecommunications applications due to its modularity. VME (Versa Module Europa) offers a robust design for military and aerospace sectors. VPX enhances VME with high-speed interconnects, ideal for demanding environments. ATCA (Advanced Telecommunications Computing Architecture) focuses on telecommunications and data centers, supporting high-performance computing. Other market types include custom boards and specialized systems tailored for niche applications.</p></p>
