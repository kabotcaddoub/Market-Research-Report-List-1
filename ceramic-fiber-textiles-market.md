<p><p>Ceramic fiber textiles are specialized materials made from aluminosilicate fibers that can withstand extremely high temperatures, typically exceeding 1,000°C (1,832°F). Emerging trends include the development of eco-friendly ceramic fibers and innovations in textile processes that enhance performance characteristics. Additionally, the demand for lightweight and high-temperature-resistant materials is growing in the aerospace and automotive sectors, propelling market dynamics.
