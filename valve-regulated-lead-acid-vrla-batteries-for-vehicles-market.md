<p><p>Valve Regulated Lead Acid (VRLA) batteries are sealed lead-acid batteries designed to prevent the escape of gases during normal operation. The two main types are Gel Cell and Absorbed Glass Mat (AGM). Gel Cell batteries use a silica gel to immobilize the electrolyte, offering deep discharge capabilities and better performance in extreme temperatures. AGM batteries utilize glass mats to absorb the electrolyte, providing high charge and discharge rates, enhanced durability, and minimal maintenance, making them popular in various vehicle applications.</p></p>
