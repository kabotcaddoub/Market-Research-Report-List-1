<p><strong>The Pharmaceutical Isolator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sterility Test Isolator</li><li>Sterility Production Isolator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical isolator market is categorized into two main types: sterility test isolators and sterility production isolators. Sterility test isolators are designed to provide a controlled environment for testing the sterility of pharmaceutical products, ensuring that they are free from microbial contamination. In contrast, sterility production isolators are utilized during the manufacturing process to maintain aseptic conditions, preventing contamination while producing sterile drugs. Both types enhance product safety and compliance with regulatory standards in the pharmaceutical industry.</p></p>
