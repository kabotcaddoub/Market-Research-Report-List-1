<p><strong>The EPVC Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Homopolymeric EPVC</li><li>Co-polymeric EPVC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EPVC resin market is primarily divided into two types: homopolymeric EPVC and co-polymeric EPVC. Homopolymeric EPVC is made from a single type of vinyl chloride polymer, offering high rigidity and excellent chemical resistance, making it suitable for applications like pipes and fittings. Conversely, co-polymeric EPVC combines different vinyl chloride monomers, enhancing flexibility and impact resistance. This type is often utilized in applications requiring a balance of durability and flexibility, such as in automotive and construction materials.</p></p>
