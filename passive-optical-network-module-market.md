<p><p>The Passive Optical Network (PON) module market includes various types such as GPON (Gigabit Passive Optical Network) and EPON (Ethernet Passive Optical Network), which enable high-speed internet access. OLT (Optical Line Terminal) manages the network, while ONU (Optical Network Unit) connects users to the network. Form factors like SFF (Small Form Factor), SFP (Small Form-Factor Pluggable), SFP+ (enhanced SFP for 10Gbps), and XFP (10Gbps transceiver) are critical in ensuring efficient data transmission and network scalability.</p></p>
