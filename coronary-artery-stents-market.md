<p><p>The coronary artery stents market consists of three main types: Bare-metal Stents (BMS), which are simple metal tubes providing structural support; Drug-eluting Stents (DES), which release medication to prevent restenosis; and Bioresorbable Vascular Scaffolds (BVS), which gradually dissolve after serving their purpose, promoting natural vessel healing. Each type addresses specific patient needs, with BMS being cost-effective, DES offering enhanced efficacy in preventing blockage, and BVS representing an innovative approach for long-term vascular health.</p></p>
