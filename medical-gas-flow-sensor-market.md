<p><p>The medical gas flow sensor market comprises various types, including flange gas flow meters and plug-in gas flow meters. Flange gas flow meters are designed for integration into fixed pipeline systems, utilizing flanged connections for secure attachment while ensuring accuracy in measuring gas flow. Conversely, plug-in gas flow meters offer greater flexibility, allowing for temporary connections and easier installation in portable applications. Both types are crucial for monitoring and regulating medical gases in healthcare settings, ensuring patient safety and compliance.</p></p>
