<p><p>Change Management Systems (CMS) can be categorized into two primary market types: Cloud-Based and On-Premise. Cloud-Based CMS solutions are hosted on external servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and remote access, making them ideal for organizations seeking quick deployment and lower upfront costs.
