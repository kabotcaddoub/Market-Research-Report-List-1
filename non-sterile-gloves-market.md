<p><p>The non-sterile gloves market comprises three primary types: latex, nitrile, and vinyl gloves. Latex non-sterile gloves offer flexibility and comfort but may cause allergic reactions in some users. Nitrile non-sterile gloves are resistant to punctures, chemicals, and provide excellent durability, making them a popular choice in various industries. Vinyl non-sterile gloves are cost-effective and suitable for low-risk tasks but lack the same level of protection as latex and nitrile. Each type serves specific needs based on usage and sensitivity.</p></p>
