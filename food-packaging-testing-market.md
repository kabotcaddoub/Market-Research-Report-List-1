<p><p>The food packaging testing market is primarily divided into physical testing and chemical testing. Physical testing evaluates the durability, strength, and integrity of packaging materials, ensuring they can withstand handling and transport. This includes assessments like tensile strength and puncture resistance. Chemical testing focuses on analyzing the interactions between food and packaging materials, checking for possible contaminants or leachables that could compromise food safety. Both testing types are crucial for ensuring compliance with safety regulations and maintaining food quality.</p></p>
