<p><p>The oil leak detection system market is divided into two main types: passive and active leak detection systems. Passive leak detection systems rely on monitoring existing equipment and environmental changes to identify leaks, often using absorbent materials or visual inspections. In contrast, active leak detection systems employ advanced technologies, such as sensors and automated monitoring, to actively detect leaks in real-time, providing immediate alerts and facilitating rapid response. Both types aim to enhance safety and minimize environmental impact in the oil industry.</p></p>
