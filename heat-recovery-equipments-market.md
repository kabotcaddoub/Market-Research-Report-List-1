<p><p>The heat recovery equipment market encompasses various types, including waste heat recovery, flash steam heat recovery, and float free condensate pumps. Waste heat recovery captures excess thermal energy from industrial processes for reuse, enhancing energy efficiency. Flash steam heat recovery involves harnessing steam generated from high-pressure water when its pressure is reduced, maximizing energy output. Float free condensate pumps efficiently collect and transport hot condensate back to boilers, minimizing energy loss and improving system reliability in thermal management applications.</p></p>
