<p><strong>The Ostomy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Colostomy</li><li>Ileostomy</li><li>Urostomy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ostomy market comprises three main types: colostomy, ileostomy, and urostomy. Colostomy involves diverting the colon to an opening in the abdomen, typically needed for patients with colorectal diseases. Ileostomy involves bypassing the entire colon, with waste collected from the small intestine, often used for inflammatory bowel disease patients. Urostomy reroutes urine from the kidneys to an external pouch, commonly necessary in cases of bladder removal. Each type requires specific products for care and management, driving the market's growth.</p></p>
