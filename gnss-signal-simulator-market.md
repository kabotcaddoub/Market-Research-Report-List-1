<p><ul><li>Single Channel Simulator</li><li>Multi Channel Simulator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GNSS signal simulator market is divided into two main types: single channel simulators and multi-channel simulators. Single channel simulators are designed to test specific GNSS receivers by replicating a single satellite signal, making them ideal for basic functionality checks. In contrast, multi-channel simulators can simulate multiple satellite signals simultaneously, allowing for comprehensive testing of GNSS devices in complex scenarios. This versatility makes multi-channel simulators essential for advanced applications such as navigation and positioning in dynamic environments.</p></p>
