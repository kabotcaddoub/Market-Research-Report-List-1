<p><p>An Adult Ventricular Assist Device (VAD) is a mechanical pump designed to support heart function and blood flow in individuals with weakened hearts. VADs help improve the quality of life and survival rates for these patients by assisting the heart in pumping blood effectively.</p><p>The Adult Ventricular Assist Device Market is witnessing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of heart diseases, rising geriatric population, and advancements in technology. <p><p>The adult ventricular assist device (VAD) market is categorized primarily into three types based on the chamber they support: left ventricle (LVAD), biventricular (BiVAD), and right ventricle (RVAD) devices. LVADs focus on aiding the left ventricle, commonly used in patients with heart failure. BiVADs support both ventricles, ideal for advanced heart failure cases. Each type caters to specific patient needs, improving circulation and quality of life while also offering different technological advancements and clinical applications.</p></p>
