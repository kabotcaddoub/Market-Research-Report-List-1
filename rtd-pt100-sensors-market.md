<p><strong>The RTD PT100 Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wire-Wound RTD</li><li>Thin Film RTD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RTD PT100 sensors market is categorized primarily into two types: wire-wound RTDs and thin film RTDs. Wire-wound RTDs consist of fine wire coiled around a ceramic or glass core, offering high accuracy and stability over a wide temperature range, making them ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, thin film RTDs utilize a thin metallic layer deposited on a substrate, resulting in a more compact design suited for applications requiring quick response times and lower costs, such as in consumer electronics.</p></p>
