<p><p>Chlorine scrubbers are essential for controlling chlorine gas emissions in industrial settings. The market is divided into two main types: dry scrubbers and wet scrubbers. Dry scrubbers utilize solid adsorbents or chemical reagents to capture chlorine gas, often leading to lower water usage and easier disposal. In contrast, wet scrubbers rely on liquid solutions to absorb chlorine, offering higher removal efficiencies but requiring more maintenance and water management. Both systems are crucial for ensuring environmental compliance and worker safety.</p></p>
