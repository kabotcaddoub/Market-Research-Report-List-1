<p><strong>The Softgel Tumble Dryer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Tumble Drying</li><li>Continuous Tumble Drying</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The softgel tumble dryer market includes two primary types: standard tumble drying and continuous tumble drying. Standard tumble drying operates in batch processes, utilizing heated air to dry softgels in specific cycles, ideal for smaller production runs. In contrast, continuous tumble drying employs a continuous flow system, where softgels are dried as they move through the dryer, accommodating larger volumes and enhancing efficiency. Both methods aim to achieve optimal moisture content while preserving the integrity of the softgel products.</p></p>
