<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Dual Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Cell Immunofluorescence Detection Market can be categorized into Single Channel and Dual Channel types. Single Channel detection involves the use of one fluorophore to visualize specific cellular targets, providing clear images of particular antigens. In contrast, Dual Channel detection employs two different fluorophores, allowing simultaneous visualization of multiple antigens, which enhances the understanding of cellular interactions and functions. Both methods are essential for research and diagnostics in fields like immunology and oncology, driving advancements in cell biology.</p></p>
