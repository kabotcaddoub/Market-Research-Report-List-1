<p><p>The Vertical-cavity Surface Emitting Laser (VCSEL) market comprises two main types: single-mode and multimode VCSELs. Single-mode VCSELs emit a single spatial mode, providing high precision and efficiency, making them ideal for applications like high-speed data communications and sensing. In contrast, multimode VCSELs can emit multiple spatial modes, allowing for higher power output and broader bandwidth, suitable for applications such as optical interconnects and data centers. Both types play crucial roles in advancing optical technologies.</p></p>
